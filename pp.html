Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26/2/2025

INTRODUCTION

ProTaxi respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the ProTaxi app.

ELIGIBILITY

  • You must be at least 18 years old to use ProTaxi.
  • Drivers must have a valid Nigerian driver's license and meet vehicle requirements.
  • Riders and drivers must provide accurate personal information during registration.
  • ProTaxi reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account that provides false information.

    • INFORMATION WE COLLECT

    Personal Information

  • Name, email, and phone number (required for account creation).
  • Payment details (stored securely by third-party payment processors).
  • Profile picture .

    • Location data

  • Real-time GPS location (for trip requests and navigation).
  • Pickup and drop-off locations.
  • We do not track your location when the app is not in use.

    • Device information

  • Device type, operating system, and app usage logs.

    • Ride history

  • Trip details, fare amounts, and driver-rider interactions.

    • HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To facilitate and improve ride-hailing services.
  • To process payments and provide receipts.
  • To ensure rider and driver safety.
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized access.
  • To provide customer support.

    • DATA SHARING & SECURITY

  • We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties.
  • Location data is only shared with the assigned driver for trip completion.
  • Payment information is encrypted and stored securely.
  • We may share data with law enforcement if required by law.

    • DATA RETENTION

  • Your account information is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Transaction records are kept for financial and security purposes.
  • You may request account deletion, but some data may be retained for legal reasons.

    • YOUR RIGHT & CHOICES

  • You can update or delete your personal information in the app.
  • You can opt out of marketing communications.
  • You can request a copy of your stored data.

    • THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • ProTaxi may integrate with third-party services such as payment providers.
  • Their privacy policies apply when using their services.

    • COOKIES & ANALYTICS

  • We use cookies to improve app functionality and personalize user experience.
  • You can disable cookies, but some features may not work properly.ProTaxi is not liable for damages, personal injury, or lost property during a trip.

    • CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

  • We may update this policy periodically.
  • Users will be notified of significant changes.
