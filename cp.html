INTRODUCTION

ProTaxi uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences. By using ProTaxi, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

WHAT ARE COOKIES

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use an app. They help us remember your preferences, improve functionality, and analyze usage patterns.

Cookies can be categorized as:

  • Essential Cookies — Necessary for app functionality and security.
  • Performance Cookies — Help us understand how users interact with our platform.
  • Functional Cookies — Store user preferences for a better experience.
  • Advertising Cookies — Used for personalized ads and marketing.

    • HOW WE USE COOKIES

    ProTaxi uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication & Security — To verify your identity, prevent fraud, and ensure account security.
  • Preferences & Personalization — To remember your ride preferences, preferred payment methods, and app settings.
  • Analytics & Performance — To track usage patterns, monitor app performance, and improve our services.
  • Advertising & Marketing — To provide relevant promotions and targeted advertisements.

    • THIRD PARTY COOKIES

    We may allow third-party services (such as analytics providers and advertisers) to place cookies on your device. These services help us analyze user behavior and show relevant ads.

    Examples of third-party services we use:

  • Google Analytics — To track and analyze user interactions.
  • Payment Processors — To facilitate secure transactions.
  • Ad Networks — To provide personalized marketing content.

    • These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies.

    MANAGING YOUR COOKIE PREFERENCES

    You have control over how cookies are used:

  • Browser Settings — You can modify your browser settings to block or delete cookies.
  • Device Settings — Mobile device settings may allow cookie restrictions.
  • Opt-Out Tools — Some third-party services provide opt-out options for tracking.

    • Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of ProTaxi and limit certain features.

    CHANGE TO THIS COOKIES POLICY

    We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated via in-app notifications or email.
